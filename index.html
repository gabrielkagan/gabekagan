I'm a student, mathematician, programmer, and investor

Hello, I'm Gabe

Freshman year of high school I joined my school's business society. I learned about finance and economics from faculty, lecturers, and peers, while also having the opportunity to research and invest in securities. I found myself captivated by the chaos and unpredictability of the markets, leading me to begin personal research and investing. My sophomore year I decided to join my school's robotics team and compete in the First Tech Challenge. Robotics was the highlight of my high school experience, inspiring me to pursue disciplines I previously did not know existed. Whether it was programming, machining, or graphic design, wherever I looked I found an opportunity to create. More than anything, I was fascinated by and found myself falling in love with the underlying mathematics.

Attending UMass Amherst, I was able to continue exploring my interests, as well as more intimately study topics of intrigue. Not only have I had the opportunity to take classes that interest me, but also to learn outside the classroom experientially. Whether through HackUMass or ASA DataFest, I have gained invaluable experience working in collaborative environments, solving real world problems.

I have worked as a web developer for a small startup, an analyst for a global bank, and in risk for a financial institution; through these experiences I have realized that my true passion lies in the mathematics.
